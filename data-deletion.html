Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 21, 2025

Share Pixel New does not collect or store any personal user data.

However, if you have authorized the App to access your Facebook or Instagram account and wish to revoke that access, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings
  2. Navigate to “Apps and Websites”
  3. Find “Share Pixel New”
  4. Click “Remove”

This will revoke the App's access to your data.
